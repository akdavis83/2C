The 2 Commandments
Christ's Summary of the Law
路 Thou shalt love the Lord thy God with all thy heart, and with all thy soul, and with all thy mind.
路 This is the first and great commandment.
路 And the second is like unto it, Thou shalt love thy neighbour as thyself.
路 On these two commandments hang all the law and the prophets.
路 (Mt 22:37-40)
